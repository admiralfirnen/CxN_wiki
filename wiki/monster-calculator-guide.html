How to Use the Calculator

1

Select Enemy Type

Choose what you want to attack from the dropdown: Normal Monsters, Rare Monsters, Strongholds, Cursed Strongholds, or Heroics.

2

Select Level

Pick the level of the enemy. Available levels depend on the enemy type you selected.

3

Select Monster Type (if applicable)

For Normal and Rare Monsters, you'll also need to select the specific monster type (Undead, Elf, Cursed, Barbarian, or Inferno Squad).

4

Look Up Battle Info

Click the button to see the recommended troops, enemy composition, rewards, and strategy tips.

Understanding the Results

Recommended Attack

The calculator shows you which troop type is best to use against the selected enemy. This is based on the enemy's troop composition and their bonuses/weaknesses.

Enemy Composition

See exactly what troops the enemy has, including their strength, health, count, and any bonus damage they deal. This helps you understand why certain troops are recommended.

Rewards

View the rewards you'll earn from defeating the enemy, including Valor Points, Experience, Master Coins (for Cursed Strongholds), and Clan Chests (for Heroics).

Troop Estimator

After looking up an enemy, you can use the Troop Estimator to calculate approximately how many of your troops you'll need to defeat the enemy.

How it Works

  1. Enter your troop type and tier (e.g., Melee Tier 6)
  2. Add any strength bonuses from research, equipment, or buffs
  3. Click "Calculate" to see the estimated troop count needed
