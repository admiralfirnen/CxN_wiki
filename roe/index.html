Rules of Engagement

These Rules of Engagement define how players and clans must interact within Kingdom 277. They are based on historical Palace rules and Senate voting. Acceptance of ROE implies acceptance of Court authority and enforcement, including enforcement by Royal Guard.

1. ROE Protection

1.1 Protected Players and Clans

  • ROE protects players who belong to clans that explicitly state in their clan description that they follow K277 ROE.
  • Clanless players and non-ROE clans are NOT protected (except where provisioned by IKFPA).
  • Non-ROE players and clans above 100,000 might may be attacked at any time, including outside Clash of Thrones (CoT).

1.2 ROE Council and Senate

  • The Council consists of the Top 100 players from the previous CoT round.
  • Members include clan leaders, clan members, Palace officials, and Royal Guard (RG).
  • New ROE amendments may be proposed and voted on by the Senate upon request by the King.

2. Taxes and Kingdom Contributions

2.1 Gold Ingots

  • Gold Ingots stored in a city's Capitol contribute to kingdom-wide bonuses.
  • All players are required to pay taxes to support kingdom growth.
  • Gold Ingots do not benefit individual cities — taxes benefit the entire kingdom.

2.2 Royal Guard (RG)

Royal Guard responsibilities include:

  • Scanning the kingdom map
  • Organizing players into training groups
  • Monitoring DM/WS chat and resolving false claims
  • Enforcing tax collection if a player/clan is unresponsive after 24 hours

3. Resources

3.1 Dragon Mounds (DM) & Wellspring Ruins (WS)

  • 1 DM and 1 WS per player per 24 hours
  • Registration is REQUIRED before marching (K277DM / K277WS chat)
  • If not claimed within 15 minutes, a new registrant may claim it
  • Unregistered or incorrect claims will be cleared by Royal Guard
  • DM/WS inside clan territory require no registration

IMPORTANT: No Gold Rush or Silver Rush unless authorized by Palace.

3.2 Attacks on DM/WS — Strike System

  • Strike 1: 2M Silver + 2 Silver per CP earned
  • Strike 2: 3M Silver + 5 Silver per CP earned
  • Strike 3: Outlawed; banned from DM/WS until Court approves appeal

3.3 Resources in Clan Territory (RSS)

ALL RSS inside clan territory belong to the clan. Unauthorized players may be forcefully removed without compensation.

3.4 Wild Resources

First come, first served.

3.5 Player-Made Resources

Only the creator may use player-made RSS. Applies inside or outside clan territory.

3.6 Player-Made Events

Belong to the clan that created them or nearest clan if outside territory.

3.7 Monsters, Epics, Arenas, Citadels, Crypts

Not considered RSS. Rare crypts opened by key belong to the player who opens them.

4. Portals (Integrated Amendment)

Memorial portals are ALWAYS off limits.

4.1 Portals During Peacetime

  • Epic and DM/WS portals off limits unless proven hostile
  • Clan territory portals ALWAYS off limits

4.2 Portals During Clash of Thrones (CoT)

  • All portals except memorial portals are valid targets during CoT.

5. Court, Violations, and Outlaws

5.1 Dispute Resolution Order

  1. Player vs Player
  2. Player vs Diplomat / Clan Leader
  3. Diplomat vs Diplomat
  4. Conflict Chat & Court

Non-ROE players cannot file cases.

5.2 Court Authority

Judges appointed by King and Chancellor. Appeals allowed within 24 hours. Rulings remain active during appeals.

5.3 Outlaw Status

Causes: ROE violations or refusal to pay compensation; harassment or hostile acts; illegal clan building attacks; severe violations.

Consequences: Loss of ROE protection; open to attack; clans sheltering outlaws risk outlaw status.

5.4 Players Below 100K Might

  • Attacks on sub-100K players are ALWAYS prohibited
  • During CoT, sub-100K may fight only among themselves
  • Third violation = OUTLAW

5.5 Clan Buildings

Attacks ALWAYS prohibited. Any attack = IMMEDIATE OUTLAW.

  • 100,000 Silver per RSS type per battle report

7. Clash of Thrones (CoT) Rules

  • Players below 100K cannot be attacked by players above 100K
  • Clan buildings are ALWAYS off limits
  • DM/WS registration rules remain active
  • All portals OTHER THAN MEMORIAL PORTALS are valid targets. Memorial portals are always off limits
  • Senate voting with Faron is FORBIDDEN
