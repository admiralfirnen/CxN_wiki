The Great Hunt

Official CxN clan strategy for The Great Hunt event in Total Battle. Follow these guidelines to maximize clan points while minimizing troop losses.

Event Overview

The Great Hunt is a clan-based tournament where players complete monster kill contracts to earn points for the clan leaderboard.

Key Mechanics

  • Each player starts with 10 contracts
  • Contracts vary by difficulty (Easy, Medium, Hard, Expert*)
  • Any monster squad that matches the contract description counts
  • Higher difficulty = more points
  • Clan ranking is based on total points earned

Expert difficulty is unlocked by manually increasing contract difficulty.

Clan Bonus Window

THIS IS THE MOST IMPORTANT SECTION

2-Hour Clan Bonus

  • The clan can activate one 2-hour bonus window
  • During this time: All contract points are DOUBLED
  • The exact activation time will be announced by leadership

Strategy

  • Save as many contracts as possible
  • Hunt primarily during the bonus window
  • Plan buffs, troops, and revives ahead of time

Contracts & Refresh Strategy

Free Refreshes

  • Each player receives 3 FREE refreshes
  • A refresh replaces your entire list of contracts

Paid Refreshes

  • Additional refreshes can be purchased with gold
  • Recommended for competitive players or strong armies

Clan Rules

  • Use all 3 free refreshes
  • Add paid refreshes if you can afford them
  • Do NOT struggle with bad or impossible contracts

Refresh vs Overextending

Do this: Refresh and complete a full new contract list.

Not this: Complete a single contract at 80–100% losses. High-loss hits destroy long-term efficiency and progress.

Contract Difficulty & Optimization

Rolling & Scaling Difficulty

  • Automatically roll Easy contracts upward
  • Target a loss range of 20–37%
    • 0–4% losses are NOT optimal unless already at Hard
  • Once you can comfortably handle Hard: roll all contracts to Expert

Difficulty Rule of Thumb

  • Losses too low → contracts too easy → wasted potential
  • Losses too high → inefficient → refresh instead

Contract Testing Order

Top-Down Testing

  • Always test contracts from the TOP of the list first
  • Goal: Complete the entire list without reviving
  • Adjust difficulty early if needed before committing troops

Troop Management & Loss Control

Loss Threshold

  • Do NOT overextend
  • Keep estimated troop losses ≤ 37%
  • High losses cost more than they gain in points

Monster Matchups & Army Composition

Counter the Monster Type

  • Always review monster strengths
  • Send troop types the monsters are NOT strong against

Optimal Army Stack Setup

Use this formation:

  • ONE LARGE stack of the optimal troop type
  • Followed by many medium stacks of the SAME troop type
  • Add monsters / mercenaries

Goal: Burst damage to wipe monsters before the next attack round. This significantly reduces losses when done correctly.

Hero, Captain & Dragon Optimization

Captain-to-Hero Ratio

VERY IMPORTANT – This ratio determines your efficiency.

Optimal ratio for The Great Hunt: 2.5 : 1 or 3 : 1 (Captain : Hero). Captains should be higher level than the Hero.

What the Ratio Means

  • Lower than 2.5:1 (Captain : Hero) → Contracts are too hard
  • Higher than 3:1 (Captain : Hero) → You lose the ability to use the Dragon for bonus leadership

Dragon Usage Rules

Dragon Is NOT Automatic

ONLY use the Dragon if it reduces loss percentage.

How to Check

  1. Select the Dragon by default
  2. Remove the Dragon
  3. Compare loss percentage:
    • If it does NOT change → leave Dragon home
    • If it does decrease losses → use Dragon

Dragon uses are limited over a long contract run. Spend them wisely.

Buffs & Boosts

Strongly Recommended – Before hunting, activate personal buffs if available:

Recommended Buffs

  • Army Strength
  • Army Health
  • Valor Points
  • Any applicable combat boosts

Buffs dramatically improve efficiency during the clan bonus window.

Rare Contracts (Optional)

  • Some players opt out of Rare contracts
  • Reason: Avoid unintentionally leveling their Hero
  • This is optional and player preference

What NOT To Do

  • Do NOT rely on reinforcements (they don't count)
  • Do NOT force impossible contracts
  • Do NOT ignore the clan bonus window
  • Do NOT burn troops for single high-loss hits
  • Do NOT waste Dragon uses without loss reduction

Clan Checklist Summary

  • Hunt during the 2-hour clan bonus
  • Use all 3 free refreshes
  • Refresh instead of taking 80–100% loss hits
  • Roll contracts to hit 20–37% losses
  • Test contracts top-down
  • Counter monster strengths with proper troop types
  • Optimize Captain : Hero = 2.5–3 : 1
  • Use Dragon only if losses decrease
  • Activate buffs before hunting

Final Note

The Great Hunt rewards discipline, optimization, and timing, not reckless force.

Follow this strategy and our clan will consistently outperform stronger but less organized clans.

Good hunting!
