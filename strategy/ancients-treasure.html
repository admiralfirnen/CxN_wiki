Ancient's Treasure

Event overview

Ancient's Treasure is an event where you complete daily quests to earn Treasure Map fragments, combine them into a map to find Vaults with Ancients' Coins, and raid those Vaults with your clan. You spend Ancients' Coins to upgrade Clan Artifacts—Tome of Knowledge, Enchanted Figurine, and Mysterious Clock—which then help your clan in the Rise of the Ancients tournament. Content below is adapted from the Total Battle Wiki.

Event flow

Rules

  • Complete Daily quests to earn Treasure Map fragments.
  • Combine the fragments into a map to find a Vault with Ancients' Coins and other rewards.
  • Complete raids on Vaults with your clanmates.
  • Reviving troops that died in Vaults is 10 times cheaper!
  • Irretrievable losses of warriors that died in Vaults have been reduced by half!
  • Upgrade Clan Artifacts using Ancients' Coins.
  • Artifacts will be useful to your Clan in defeating enemies in the Rise of the Ancients tournament. (Always after this tournament)
  • Artifact progress will reset after the end of the Rise of the Ancients tournament.
  • You can't complete the Treasure Map if you joined your Clan after the start of the tournament.

Clan artifacts

Three artifacts are upgraded with Ancients' Coins: Tome of Knowledge, Enchanted Figurine, and Mysterious Clock. Their bonuses apply in the Rise of the Ancients tournament.
