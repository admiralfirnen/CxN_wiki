Rise of the Ancients

Tournament overview

Rise of the Ancients is the tournament that runs after Ancient's Treasure. The three Clan Artifacts you upgrade during Ancient's Treasure—Tome of Knowledge, Enchanted Figurine, and Mysterious Clock—apply here: they boost Valor/Experience/tournament points from epic monster squad battles, increase how many epic squads you can summon, and increase the time allowed to defeat them. Content below is adapted from the Total Battle Wiki (Ancients' Treasure).

How the artifacts help you

  • Valor, Experience & tournament points — Earn more from battles against epic monster squads. Upgrade levels 1–28; bonus from +0% up to +80%.
  • Epic monster squad summons — Unlock more summons (1–25 depending on level). Upgrade levels 1–12.
  • Time for defeating epic squads — More time per battle, from 2m at level 1 up to 120m at level 28.

All three artifacts are upgraded with Ancients' Coins during the Ancient's Treasure event. Plan your upgrades there so your clan is ready for Rise of the Ancients.
