Recommended Talent Point Order

Apply Talent points in this order:

  1. Guardsmen Strength
  2. Guardsmen Health
  3. Specialist Strength
  4. Specialist Health
  5. Monster Strength
  6. Monster Health

Pro Tip

Note: Every time your hero levels up, try to assign a point to Economy or Archeology.
